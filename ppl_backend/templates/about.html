About Us

Who We Are

Personnel Practice provides human resource management solutions to businesses. It currently recruits for clients across the manufacturing, retail and mining sectors and has about 2,000 employees under management for these clients. We specialize in Executive Recruitment Services and Managed Employee Services (Agency).

Leadership

Yaw Benneh-Amponsah
CEO

Yaw Benneh-Amponsah is the Executive Director of Personnel Practice. He has over 20 years experience in Human Resource Management and Finance. Prior to founding Personnel Practice, he served as an HR Officer with Stanley Mining Services Ltd and West African Drilling Services Ltd, and then served as Chief Finance Officer with Geodrill Limited with responsibility for all non-operational functions including human resources. He holds a BSc Business Administration from the University of Ghana and PGDip Financial Strategy from Said Business School, Oxford University, UK.
